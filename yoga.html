Workouts/Yoga

Warm Up

Warming up is very crucial to any form of exercise. It prevents from hurting yourself too hard and cramps. Play the video and try to copy the moves for about 10 repetitions on your own.

Yoga

Now that your body is warmed up and ready to go for real exercises. Let's do some yoga together. Try achieving the position as presented in the video slowly, and hold it for 30 seconds. Remember, don't hurt yourself if this is too difficult.

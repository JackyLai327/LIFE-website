Meditation

Meditating is a way to clear your minds and brings you a lighter mood. By listening to the nature while meditating, you can feel more relaxed and peaceful.

Now, find a sweet spot to meditate without being bothered. Sit down with your leg crossed, and gently place your hands on your knees in a comfortable angle. When you close your eyes later, try not to think about anything at all, and focus only on the sounds of the nature. You may now close your eyes and enjoy your inner peace.

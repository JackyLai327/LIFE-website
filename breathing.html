4-7-8 Breathing Technique

4-7-8 breathing technique is a special breathing practice which you will need to control your breath, both inhaling and exhaling. This breathing technique preserves more oxygen that you inhale, thus giving an oxygen boost to your blood and muscles. It is often compared to mindfulness meditation. Once you know how to do it, you are more than welcome to combine 4-7-8 breathing and meditation to create a more peaceful world inside your minds.

Here's the official steps of doing 4-7-8 breathing:

  1. First, let your lips part. Make a whooshing sound, exhaling completely through your mouth.
  2. Next, close your lips, inhaling silently through your nose as you count to four in your head.
  3. Then, for seven seconds, hold your breath.
  4. Make another whooshing exhale from your mouth for eight seconds.

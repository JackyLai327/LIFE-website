L.I.F.E.

COVID-19 pandemic has caused people around the globe a huge impact. It changes how people used to live and the way people interact. Stemmed from this pandemic, many people had to quarantine themselves from who they love. This is causing many people to lose the ability to maintain fit and healthy, to socailise, and some even worse, to stay happy. People are reaching out for help with mental and psychological wellbeing.

Here on L.I.F.E., which is short for Living It Fully Everyday, we dedicate to help people to practice mental and physical health. With paid registration, anyone can enjoy all our unlimited tutorial videos that enhance their mental stability by doing Yoga, meditation, deep breathing, and many more.

Beyond all the great services we provide on our website, there is also loads of resources regarding the corona virus pandemic that will take you directly to number of cases, Australian Government Department of Health, articles explaining corona virus and COVID-19, and much more.
